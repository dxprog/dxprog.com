One of their so called "storez" was Amazon.com (one of the things that got me ineterested in the first place), but here's is Amazon's reply to my inquiry about GreenZap:

[quote]"At this time, we do not accept GreenZap as a payment method on our web site.

Also, we have no plans to accept GreenZap as a payment method in the future. We have asked GreenZap to remove the Amazon.com logo from their site."[/quote]
